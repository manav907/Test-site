Delicate Gut

Metabolism controls how efficiently your body processes and absorbs nutrients. A delicate gut affects digestive function.

Gut Motility Disorder

Your intestines move food in a rhythmic way. Any imbalance in this movement disrupts digestion.

Gut-Brain Miscommunication

Your brain and gut are constantly in sync. When communication is off, it impacts digestion and gut sensitivity.

Stress-Triggered Digestive Dysfunction

Stress directly affects gut function by altering digestion speed, enzyme activity, and bacterial balance.

Sensitive Intestinal Lining

Your intestinal lining acts as a protective barrier, allowing nutrients to be absorbed while blocking harmful substances. When it becomes sensitive, it leads to gut issues.

Compromised Enzyme Release

Digestive enzymes break down food. If they aren't functioning properly, food remains undigested, affecting gut health.

Stomach Mucus Barrier Disruption

A thin mucus layer leaves your gut exposed, making it more vulnerable to external irritants and bacteria.
